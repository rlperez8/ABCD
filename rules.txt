=====Goose=====

ABCD: 
- D must be touching or near a clear trend line.
Trend Line:
- Must touch at least 3 seperate candle wicks.



Top Goose → a reversal from resistance (market high)
- A high must be all time candle high.



Bottom Goose → a reversal from support (market low or ongoing trend support)

Q: Is Goose the high of all candles? 
A: No

Q: Is Goose the high of past year candles? 
A: No

Q: Is Goose support line bottom or top of previous candles?
A: Bottom
